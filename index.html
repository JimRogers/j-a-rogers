About

Front End Developer / SEO Specialist

With over a decade of front-end development experience, I specialize in building high-quality, user-centric digital solutions for businesses of all sizes. My expertise centers on CMS platforms like WordPress and HubSpot, where I create bespoke websites, custom modules, and flexible templates tailored to both user needs and business goals.

As a creative front-end developer, I design intuitive, visually engaging interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—ensuring seamless experiences and strong engagement on any device. My approach combines a keen eye for design with technical precision, resulting in websites that are both beautiful and highly functional.

In addition to my development skills, I am a seasoned SEO specialist. I leverage tools such as Google Analytics 4 (GA4), Google Tag Manager (GTM), and Semrush for in-depth keyword research, share of voice analysis, and position tracking. This allows me to optimize every website I build for search performance, driving more organic traffic and supporting broader marketing strategies.

Recently, I have been focused on optimizing website content for large language models (LLMs), ensuring visibility and performance within AI-driven search and summarization tools. My holistic approach to web development—combining front-end creativity, CMS mastery, and technical SEO—enables me to deliver scalable, lead-generating digital solutions that accelerate business growth.
